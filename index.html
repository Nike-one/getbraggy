      Brag Score vs ATS Score

      ATS score = keyword match rate + parsing compatibility (machine check).
      Brag Score = recruiter-perception score (human quality check).

      Specificity · 0-25
      Numbers, percentages, named tools in bullets
      Action · 0-25
      Strong verbs vs "responsible for" / passive voice
      Outcome · 0-25
      Results stated vs plain task lists
      Density · 0-25
      Signal-to-noise — filler and buzzwords purged

      You could pass ATS at 90% and score 30/100 on Brag if your bullets are all "responsible for maintaining systems." Two completely different questions.

      Questions Indian Professionals Ask

      Is Braggy really a free résumé review?

      Yes — completely free, no sign-up. Drop your résumé and you'll get a Brag Score, red flags, and a recruiter-style verdict in about 10 seconds. Drop your email to download your rewritten résumé as a PDF.

      Is this an ATS resume checker for Naukri and LinkedIn?

      Sort of — but Braggy goes further than a typical ATS score checker or Naukri FastForward parser. We don't just count keywords. We tell you why your résumé reads junior, where you sound replaceable, and which bullets are pulling down your market value.

      Will Braggy write my résumé for me?

      No. Braggy is not a résumé writing service in India. We tell you what's wrong with your résumé and show specific rewrites for your weakest bullets. You make the changes — we tell you exactly what to change and why.

      What roles and cities does Braggy work for?

      Mid-career Indian professionals with 2–10 years of experience — product, engineering, marketing, design, operations, finance, consulting. Tailored to the Indian job market across Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Chennai.

      How is this different from Naukri FastForward or other ATS score checkers?

      Naukri FastForward and most ATS checkers give you a parsing-rate number and a list of missing keywords. Braggy gives you the recruiter's verdict — your perceived salary band, where you sound less experienced than you are, and what to fix before your next switch.

      Will my résumé data be stored or shared?

      No. Your résumé is processed by AI for this analysis only and discarded immediately after. We don't store résumé text, sell data, or share it with recruiters — and our AI provider doesn't train on your résumé either. See our privacy policy.
